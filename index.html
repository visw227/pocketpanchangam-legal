Privacy Policy — PocketPanchangam (పాకెట్ పంచాంగం)

Effective date: July 5, 2026

PocketPanchangam ("the app") is a Telugu panchangam, festival calendar, and Bhagavad Gita app. This policy explains what information the app handles and why. Summary: no account, no sign-in, and your location never leaves your device.

Information we collect

DataPurposeWhere it goes
Approximate / precise location (optional)Computing sunrise, sunset, and panchangam timings for your placeProcessed on your device only. Never transmitted to us or anyone else.
App usage events (screens viewed, sessions), device model, OS versionUnderstanding how the app is used, improving features (Google Analytics for Firebase)Google (Firebase Analytics)
Push token / topic subscriptionDelivering festival greetings and app announcements you can disable in system settingsGoogle (Firebase Cloud Messaging)
Advertising identifier (IDFA/AAID), coarse ad signalsShowing one banner-style ad per screen (Google AdMob). On iOS we ask permission via App Tracking Transparency; if you decline, ads are non-personalized.Google (AdMob)

What we do NOT collect

  • No name, email, phone number, or account of any kind
  • No contacts, photos, files, or messages
  • No location history — location is read transiently to compute timings and is not stored or sent

Notifications

Daily shloka, Rahu Kalam alerts, and festival reminders are scheduled locally on your device. Optional broadcast messages (e.g., festival greetings) are delivered through Firebase Cloud Messaging. You can disable all of these in the app's Settings or your device settings at any time.

Third-party services

The app uses Google services whose handling of data is governed by their own policies: Google Privacy Policy, Firebase Privacy, and AdMob.

Data retention & your choices

  • Analytics data is retained per Firebase's standard retention (14 months) and is aggregated/pseudonymous.
  • iOS: decline tracking in the App Tracking Transparency prompt (Settings → Privacy → Tracking) for non-personalized ads.
  • Android: reset or delete your advertising ID in Settings → Google → Ads.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored preferences and scheduled notifications.

Children

The app is a general-audience devotional/reference app and is not directed at children under 13. Ad content is restricted to Google's "G" (general audiences) rating.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves (for example, if new features are added). The effective date above will change accordingly.
